Game Overview

Take Turns

The game goes player by player, with each player taking a single action.

Gain Prestige

Projects grant discounts and sometimes Prestige. When you meet an Executive's discount requirements, you claim that Executive and gain additional Prestige. Executives are first come, first served, and once claimed, they cannot change hands.

Win The Office

The first player to hit 15 prestige triggers the beginning of the end. Any player who hasn't gotten a turn that round yet will get a turn. At the end of the round, the player with the most prestige wins.

On Your Turn

Take Resources

Take 3 different resources from the resource bank, or 2 of the same kind if there are 4 or more of that kind available. You cannot hold more than 10 resources at once (including executive favor).

Claim Project

Call dib's on a project and move it to your workboard. It's not done, but no one else can do it. This will gain you executive favor if any remain. You can only have 3 projects on your workboard at a time.

Complete Project

If you have the combined resources (executive favor acts as a wildcard) and discounts, you can complete a project. Completing a project grants you a discount corresponding to the project and grants you that projects prestige.
